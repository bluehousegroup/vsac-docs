Roster verification

Roster for Budget 2019 - Semester 1 - 2018-2019

To verify enrollment for another academic year, use the Active Year dropdown located above.

Instructions

  • Verify and update (if necessary) each student's financial aid and enrollment status
  • Tuition Waivers, other than those for students enrolled in an Early College/Vast program should be reported in the Other Gift Aid field. VA Benefits received should also be reported in the Other Gift Aid field.
  • After verifying a student's financial aid and enrollment data, check the "Verification Complete" box.
  • If you are unable to verify a student, leave the "Verification Complete" box blank. The student will remain on your verification roster.
  • To save your changes for the students on the page, click the "Save and Refresh" button. If the "Verification Complete" box was checked, the student will no longer appear on the roster.
  • To refresh the screen without saving any changes, click the Refresh Without Saving button

Positioning form

The results view has a quick positioning form at the top. The form uses the form-inline class which enables Flexbox allowing us to use Flexbox utility classes like justify-content-center to center align the form. Spacing classes like mb-2 are used to adjust the spacing around the form.

<!-- Positioning form -->
<div class="form-inline justify-content-center mb-2">
  <div class="form-group">
    <label for="last-name">Position to applicant last name &nbsp;</label>
    <input type="text" class="form-control form-control-sm" name="last-name" id="last-name">
    <button type="submit" class="btn btn-sm btn-secondary btn-auto">Go</button>
  </div>
</div>

Pagination

The pagination appears the top of the results and is repeated at the bottom. Flexbox utility classes are used to align the Previous/Next buttons and the page number.

Buttons can have a disabled attribute to grey them out and disable mouse interactions. If links are used, they can have a class="disabled" to get the same effect.

</div>
